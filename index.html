Useless Machine

Useless cover version of an eye chart. This generates a new experience by encouraging viewers to search for hidden element (E facing on the other side) to only see none as it is actually a completely useless eye test.

Escape

An exploration of how the visual can embed meaning into the word's graphic representation. 'Escape' can be defined as 'fail to be noticed.' Using this aspect of the word, a 30"x30" puzzle was created for viewers to find the word 'escape' which exclusively existed among the letters E, S, C, A and P.

SHADE

Shade is a detergent brand created as a defamiliarization of laundry experience. Detergent is used for the purpose of cleaning and removing stain. Instead, Shade, is intended to add charcoal stain to your clothes. The product is marketed towards customers interested in art as black clothing is usually associated with artists.

LO-RES

Type specimen poster and book on Zuzana Licko's Lo-Res families. Lo-Res is a hybrid typeface between bitmap representation of letters and pixelated designs. As Lo-Res families are composed of different width but has the same x-height typeface, this type specimen poster and book are made by layering each typeface size to show the higher the number of Lo-Res, the narrower the font is.

THE MAN WITH THE GOLDEN ARM

The Man with the Golden Arm's opening title gives a rebirth in film by using simplified type of design of basic colors and geometric shapes. This poster contains information on the film, screencaps of titles and its iconic arm. The design is composed with rectangular shapes in black and white color scheme, the same used in the opening title.
